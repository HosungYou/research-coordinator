## Action Research Cycle

```
     PLAN
       ↓
    ┌──────┐
    │ ACT  │
    └──────┘
       ↓
    OBSERVE
       ↓
    REFLECT
       ↓
    (Repeat)
```

## CBPR Principles

1. Community as unit of identity
2. Build on strengths
3. Collaborative partnership
4. Co-learning and capacity building
5. Cyclical and iterative process
6. Address health from ecological perspective
7. Disseminate findings to all partners
8. Long-term commitment
9. Balance research and action
